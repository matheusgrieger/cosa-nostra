The game Cosa Nostra

This location-based game allows you to take over real-world establishments to build your gangster empire and become the kingpin of your city.

Build your strategy, battle agains other players to become more experienced and wealthier, hire thugs or steal from them.

Every place you conquer is connected to the real place by Foursquaretm

The more popular a place is the more money it will generate to you.

See the map of your own town in a diferente perspective.

Every place you can get into is a deal you may fight for.

Cosa Nostra Features

  • Conquer

    Earn the money, conquer your business and fight for the respect!

  • Recruit

    Not really in a popular area? Now's a great time to recruit some new henchmen that might be near you, they surely can help you out.

  • Skills

    Build-up your character as you gain experience focusing in the skills you find more important for your strategy.

  • Training

    Let your henchmen hit the gym or the shooting range, training them to enhance your chances of winning.

  • Interact

    Take over the world by going outside, or do that within the confort of your home. It's your call.

  • Compete

    Earn your friends', your town's or even the world's respect.
