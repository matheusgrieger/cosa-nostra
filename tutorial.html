Cosa Nostra

Game Guide

Welcome to the 20’s gangsters’ world of Cosa Nostra. We are glad to have you as an early adopter. Because we didn’t finish the in-game tutorials yet we are sending this email with some important instructions to help you in the game.

Your objective is to make the “family business” prosper and to become the city’s kingpin. Recruit henchmen from the streets, fight for new businesses in the real world and pull out your best tactics to achieve that.

  • Recruitment

  1. Localization

    Look around your localization to see allthese highlights.

    Each of these are properties that might generate profit to your business. Build a platoon of your henchmen to buy this business with bullets.

    These are the citizens of this town which you can interact. The hat and tie show you that they are already a part of the criminal world. Those without it are civilians, which also have their part in the game. When you find them you usually have two choices: loot or recruit them to work for you.

  2. Recruitment

    This screen is like the recruitment agency of the mafia world. Choose among the options on the list to build your crew. Each squad you recruit can have up to 20 henchmen.

  3. Venues

    The establishments in the city are divided in 14 levels. The greater the level is, the greater the income will be and probably the stronger will be de defenses. The difficulty is calculated regarding the proportion between the defenses and your strongest henchmen available.

    You can choose up to six squads of henchmen to invade. But have in mind that the more you use the more energy you will need. The distance you are from the establishment will also influence in the energy cost. Nearer venues are easier to attack. And finally, try not to use excessive strength in the invasion, because the earned XP is relative to the battle difficulty.

  4. Properties

    Every property you own generates a certain amount of profit per day. You’re able to collect the income of all your properties all together in the profile. Although it is also possible to collect one by one to receive a 20% bonus, but for that you must be really near the establishment.

    Also, don’t forget to put some of your henchmen to defend your properties or they will be at your enemies’ disposal.

  5. Family

    From all the henchmen in your Family, only those that are not defending any property can be used in battle.

    To make your family even stronger you can expend some energy to train your squads. With it their stats are raised temporarily. You can also invest some ducats to upgrade your henchmen permanently.

  6. Skills

    As a player you are able to among three different skills to improve: Energy, Income and Training. By each level you gain you will be rewarded with upgrade points, which can be used to raise your level in each of these skills.

    Energy

    Focus on having more energy to perform you attacks and other actions suited. The more energy you have more quickly you’ll be able to react to the threats and opportunities presented to you.

    Training

    If you like to prepare your team before a heist, then investing in training might just be your thing. The more you invest in this skill the more your henchmen will receive as training bonuses.

    Income

    It is undeniable that money is what makes the world go round. In that case this skill you’ll help you raise the most possible in the smallest time. As you power it up you receive greater bonuses on your income collects.
